Gesture FX

Two hands, two jobs. Left hand shapes the raw sound — pitch, distortion, filter, noise, vibrato. Right hand shapes the space — reverb, delay, volume. Each hand only needs three shapes.

LEFT · sound

  • ✊ Fist — height: pitch · rotate: distortion
  • 🖐 Open — height: filter · L/R: noise
  • ✌ Peace — height: vibrato depth · rotate: speed

RIGHT · space

  • ✊ Fist — height: reverb size · rotate: wet/dry
  • 🖐 Open — height: volume · L/R: delay feedback
  • ☝ Point — angle: delay time · height: delay level

Both fists = reset · Both palms raised high = swell
